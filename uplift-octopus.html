A fanbased addition to the project "Eclipse Phase", from Posthuman Studios.

What is an Uplift Octopus?

An uplift Octopus is a fictional sapient creature in the Universe of Eclipse Phase. Uplift is the process of altering an animal and making it sapient and self-aware.

This game is big and complex, and the universe is explained through 14 dense books.

Brief History

The first Uplift creatures were created decades before the Fall (Eclipse Phase is somehow a post-apocalyptic game, and the Fall would refer to the moment in which the Earth became non habitable) by big corporations. Controversy was served. Was that moral? Do they have rights?

Some time later, Uplift Octopus were created.

Personality and Society

Original octopus are lonely predators, and this reflected in the personality of the uplifted version of this creature.

Cautious, reserved, tactical, manipulative... Uplift Octopus spend the majority of their lives in Isolation, and their interactions are usually strictly professional.

However, they are an active part of the Mercurials, a group of activist that fight for the rights of non-human sapient creatures, such as themselves.
